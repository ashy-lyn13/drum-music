Drum Beats

Here is a collection of drum beats. From beginner to advanced, there is a lot of content to work through here. Each set of exercises has a technical theme and I've categorized them by a general idea of difficulty.
These should be played with the right hand on high hat, ride, and floor tom. It's very important to practice these at different tempos(speed) and to repeat them at least 8 times without stopping.
It's also really helpful to link beats together and switch between them in 4-16 bar loops. If you have a hard time visualizing this, I have some patterns in the section "Theory and Practice Patterns" that should make this a little easier.

Theory and Practice Patterns

Is your theory a little rusty? I have made some basic theoretical overview sheets that explain reading drum notation, how to read different types of notes, and how to make practical lead sheets. Music theory does not need to be a daunting and oppressive set of rules, it can be very helpful for structuring our learning and communicating our ideas to other musicians.
The practice patterns in this section are helpful for combining the different exercise sheets with one another in ways that you will see in many songs that you will learn.
